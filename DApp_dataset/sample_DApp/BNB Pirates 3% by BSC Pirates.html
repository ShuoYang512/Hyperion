BNB Pirates Info

BNB Pirates pays 3% daily according to the current mining efficiency rate. Objective of the game is hiring more pirates, sooner and more often than other players. This in turn will make you earn BNB faster. Compounding your daily BNB earnings will 3x your miners within 30 days or less.

How To Mine Effectively

1) Buy Pirates with your BNB.
2) Your Pirates will start mining and placing the BNB they find into your chest.
3) Compound the BNB in your chest to increases your mining efficiency rate.
4) Alternatively, you can collect the BNB in your chest.
