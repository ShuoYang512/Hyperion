FAQS WHAT IS METASTAR STRIKERS? + MetaStar Strikers is a Web3 game that combines football with a fun gameplay, allowing its players to own their assets and trade them with their friends. All in a Score &amp; Own unique way. HOW DO I GET THE DIGITAL COLLECTIBLES? + In the next few weeks we will announce the Pre Sales for our Strikers. You will need to own either a Mint Pass or a Free Claim to participate in the Pre Sale! Stay tuned for more details to grab your Founders Edition Digital Collectibles! WHAT IS $MSTAR AND BL? + $MSTAR is the governance token of the game and BL is the in-game currency, where players can use it in different ways like accelerating Striker evolution or purchasing in-game packs. WHAT IS THE VISION FOR METASTAR? + The MetaStar Metaverse will begin with a football game, and will then expand to other sports like basketball, tennis, or golf, unlocking a fully interoperable metaverse of tokens and digital collectibles.
