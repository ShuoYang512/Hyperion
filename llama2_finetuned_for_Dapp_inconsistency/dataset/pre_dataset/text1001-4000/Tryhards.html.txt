BOX is an ERC-1155 standard NFT. Each week there will be 1 BOX Raffle. How much does a box cost? Tryhards boxes are pegged to 100 TRY tokens. How does a player purchase a ticket? Players can purchase boxes depending on their amount of gTRY. When purchasing a box they lock in 100 TRY for each (chance) ticket they want. 1 Ticket can be locked for free every week. In case the player wants to purchase multiple tickets, TRY token needs to be staked to increase their amount of gTRY. When the raffle has ended players pay for the number of tickets they won and can claim back the amount of TRY that might be left for the tickets they did not win. Players receive a loot box NFT for each ticket they have won. The lootbox raffle The weekly raffle ends at 23:59 CET and at 00:00 Monday morning a new raffle starts that will end the following Sunday at 23:59. Giving the players 1 week to sign up. Players can sign up for the raffle at the latest 30 minutes before the raffle starts. How many loot boxes are distributed each raffle? The amount of loot boxes that are being distributed depends on how many tickets were bought for the specific raffle. 20% of the purchased tickets get rewarded with a box. gTRY Ticket Brackets Amount of gTRY a player holds Number of (chances) tickets to win a box &lt;2999 1 &gt;3000 3 &gt;15000 15 &gt;30000 25 &gt;75000 40 &gt;300000 60 &gt;500000 100 Will the requirements change? Depending on the situation, gTRY requirements may change. The raffle starts off with a 20% chance and may be capped in the future when too many players participate. Using Smart Contracts for the Raffle is prohibited. BOX Raffle rules will be decided by gTRY voting in the future.
