 Winter Bears Merch ADOPT ME Welcome to Winter Bears Winter Bears is a collection of 10,000 adorable polar bear NFTs available on the Ethereum blockchain. Each bear is completely unique, created using over 150 individual features. Rarity Each Winter Bear is special, but some are even more special than the rest. From santa hats to sleeping eyes, all Winter Bears attributes have a different rarity score. The combination of features each bear has builds its' total rarity score and overall rarity tier. Aurora tier are the rarest bears, showcasing the most unique features. There are also five extra special unique bears to watch out for. If you find one of these, make sure to let us know!
